Timeline of Matt Shea and White Nationalism

 
 

November 2008

Elected to State House every two years since 2008. Serves as assistant ranking minority member on the House’s Labor and Workforce and Judiciary committees.

November 2011

Charged in December with a single misdemeanor count of having a loaded handgun in a vehicle without a valid permit after he allegedly pulled a handgun on another driver during a road rage incident in November 2011.

August 2012

Shea exposes his political opponent's house on social media. Shea goes even further to reveal his opponent’s, Amy Biviano, address by listing the cross streets near her home. Shea posted this photo with the following caption: “I wanted to give a special thanks to all of those in the newest 4th District Precinct ....Thank you all for the overwhelming show of support, what a great neighborhood! Oh...and that's my opponent's house in the background. =).”

July 2013

Shea addresses attendees at the patriot rally about the ultimate collapse of government. He speaks to the crowd in Farragut State Park and told them “to get organized and to prepare for when the government steps in after the ‘inevitable collapse.'“

Shea also urged them to stock up on thousands of rounds of ammunition and to stay in shape, practice shooting, and learn self defense and special tactics, according to COEUR d’ALENE/Post Falls Press.

April 2014

Shea travels to Nevada to support Cliven Bundy in a standoff against the Bureau of Land Management (BLM). Shea lead a coalition of state legislators, sheriffs, other representatives and leaders in the so-called “patriot community” to announce their new alliance, the Coalition of Western States (COWS). The purpose of this organization is to support individuals like Cliven Bundy who provoke the federal government into a standoffs over disputes about the authority of the BLM.

November 2014

Shea gets re-elected to represent the 4th Legislative District, despite his role in supporting Cliven Bundy’s armed standoff with the federal government.

January 2016

Shea travels to Oregon to support Ammon Bundy, son of Cliven Bundy, in an armed stand-off against the Bureau of Land Management. He decides to take this stand despite this placing him in direct opposition to the FBI, disregarding the chance of escalating the possibility of armed conflict between the group and federal agents.

June 2016

Shea founds Spokane chapter of Act for America. Act for America is the largest anti-Muslim group in the nation. Led by Brigitte Gabriel, Act for America is a listed by the non-partisan Southern Poverty Law Center as a hate group.

December 2016 - January 2017

Shea sponsors a Bill in the the State House in efforts to separate Eastern Washington from the rest of the state to create Liberty State. Though he denies it, leaked copies of a subsequent document tied to the Liberty State movement that Shea leads outlines a radical overhaul of the new state’s constitution to “glorify Jesus Christ” and limit immigration of all kinds to maintain the existing percentage of the population for each minority group.

June 2018

Shea headlines Marble Country’s 24th annual God and Country Celebration in Marble, WA. Shea said the reason America was no longer a “beacon of Christianity”, was “because of compromise. And it’s not knowing that the communists are training, they’re planning, they’re organizing and they are lying in wait.” Shea’s speech ignited the crowd to be ready for another civil war to fight “communists” despite no evidence that any communists are active in Eastern WA.

October 2018

Shea’s Biblical Basis of War document is leaked, condemning abortion and same-sex marriage while advocating for violent conversion of non-believers if necessary:

“Under one heading, ‘Rules of War,’ it makes a chilling prescription for enemies who flout ‘biblical law.’ It states, ‘If they do not yield – kill all males.’”

Read Shea’s complete manifesto here.

Shea’s extremist rhetoric gets national attention through Vice News and other media outlets.

November 2018

Corporate donors begin to pull their financial support from Shea’s campaign and ask for refunds of campaign donations. During this time Avista Corp., the BNSF Railway Co., AT&T, Washington Realtors, Northwest Credit Union Association divested from Shea’s campaign.

July 2019

The Washington State House hires a firm to investigate Rep. Matt Shea to determine his involvement in political violence.

August 2019

Republican Spokane County Sheriff Ozzie Knezovich raises concerns about Shea conducting background checks on his political opponents. The Republican Sheriff was so concerned about Shea’s behavior that he says he turned over materials about Shea to the FBI and called Shea an “embarrassment to the Republican Party.”

Knezovich elaborated: “When you take a look at the context of those communications, this isn't about Matt being worried about death threats. This is about him, and his organization, and those around him targeting political enemies."

August 2019

Shea’s extremism reaches a new level when he joins Team Rugged to support the training of young boys and men for “biblical warfare”. Rep. Shea promotes the use of firearms as core to "returning to patriot principles" — recklessly encouraging his most unstable supporters to take matters into their own hands by using rhetoric like “restoring patriotism” and “Christian warfare”.

Many organizations and officials called for Shea’s resignation, after videos surfaced showing Team Rugged’s militia styled training for young boys and men.

August 2019

Leaked emails surface regarding Shea’s support for this organization training children for holy war. One of the group’s leader shared that the purpose of Team Rugged “is to provide patriotic and biblical training on war for young men,” in the series of emails with Shea.

Where We Stand Today

THE FIGHT CONTINUES! Shea is under investigation for allegedly advocating for violence and surveillance against his political adversaries with anti-government extremists. A preliminary report of the House’s investigation will be disclosed to House leadership of both parties on September 30, 2019. An extensive report of the investigation will be released in December 2019. House Minority Leader J.T. Wilcox refuses to comment on Shea’s actions and should take fault as the State’s Republican leader!

The fight to remove Matt Shea from the State House continues!
